DegreeView Excel Templates

Excel themes are created using a lengthy python file which can be configured to adjust a variety of variables including background colors, fonts, font sizes, and font colors, border colors, row and column widths, and more. By combining many different configurations an Excel file can take an unlikely form, as an art canvas.

Currently there are 10 themes on the site, with over 2500 unique downloadable files. Many more themes are planned to come soon.
